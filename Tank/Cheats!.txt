CHEATS:

When in the pause menu, type in the following without quotes and press enter to get the specified result:

"mostimpressive" : Instantly beat the level.
"chessburger" : Acess the laser gun, hold space to fire.
"cls" : Turns off screen clearing. May cause siesures.
"part" : Turns off particle drawing and updating, but not creation. Hint: Use in comination with "cls" for interesting results.
